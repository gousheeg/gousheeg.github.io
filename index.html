Overview:

Forza Horizon 4 is a racing/driving game developed by Playground Games and Turn10 Studios and published by Microsoft Studios. Unlike the racing only focused Forza Motorsport, Forza Horizon 4 brings a much more fun and relaxed experience to the table. Apart from the racing aspect of the game there is also a free roam mode for those just looking to relax and enjoy driving their dream cars. The game also offers a community where one can create and share custom paint designs, custom tunes as well photos taken in-game. Ever since its debut in October of 2018, the game has received regular updates adding more content ranging from cars to community desgined races along with two major expansion packs: Fortune Island and LEGO Speed Champions. A recent update also added a battle royale mode called the Eliminator.

Fortune Island:

Fortune Island was the first expansion for Forza Horizon 4. Released in December 2018, it brought a new map with mountainous terrain and extreme weather alongside some new vechicles. The expansion involved a progressive gameplay with riddles which revealed the location of treasure chests. The expansion also boasts the games longest drift zone.

LEGO Speed Champions:

The LEGO Speed Champions expansion was the second one to be released for this game. With its release in June 2019, this expansion added a new world modelled by LEGO bricks along with five LEGO vehicles which can be unlocked as the player progressed through the game.
