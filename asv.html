Lamborghini
Aventador Super Veloce LP750-4

Overview:

Introduced at the 2015 Geneva Motor Show, the Aventador SV is the successor to the original Aventador. It features a re-designed front splitter and rear diffuser with more angular and aggressive styling when compared to those of the original Aventador. The vehicle utilised more carbon fibre incorporated into the chassis to reduce weight and featured serveral improvements in the suspension, steering and powertrain departments. It was available in two versions: the coupe and the roadster with production limited to 600 units for the coupe and 500 units for the roadster model.
Within the game, this vehicle was one of the rarest to obtain since it was a wheelspin prize. But towards the end of 2019 it was made available as a prize for weekly events after which many units are sold in the auction house but still at quite a high price.
